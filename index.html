A little about B1-B2 VISA

The B1/B2 visa is a non-immigrant visa category that allows individuals to temporarily visit the United States for business (B1) or tourism/pleasure (B2). These visas are commonly issued for short trips, such as attending meetings, conferences, or training sessions (B1), or for leisure travel, visiting friends or family, and exploring tourist attractions (B2).
